Why do you want to take this course?

I would like to take this course because front-end development is the foundation of creating user-friendly, interactive websites and applications. I am passionate about designing engaging digital experiences, and believe mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript will give me the ability to take creative ideas and make them work functionally.

What do you aim to learn by the end of this course?

By the end of this course, I hope to have a thorough knowledge base about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and their collaborative role in creating responsive and visually appealing websites. Besides, I would like to learn about best practices in coding and structuring web pages and improving performance. Also, I am looking forward to gaining practical experience by completing projects that will be useful in proving my ability to develop real-world applications.

How do you think this course will benefit you in your future goals?

This course will benefit me by providing the technical foundation necessary for a career in web development. As front-end skills are highly in demand, learning them will open up opportunities in the tech industry. Additionally, understanding how to build user-friendly interfaces will be valuable as I explore more advanced topics like frameworks, back-end development, or even UX/UI design. It will also boost my confidence in working on freelance projects or collaborating with teams.
